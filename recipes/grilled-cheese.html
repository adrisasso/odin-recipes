Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese recipe - the perfect comfort food!

This recipe has simple ingredients but tons of flavor with lots of cheese.

ingredients

  • Sliced cheese
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter slices of bread
  2. Add cheese slices between two slices of bread.
  3. Place sandwich on pan on hot stove.
  4. Toast until cheese is melted.
